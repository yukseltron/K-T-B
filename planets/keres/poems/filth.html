Filth

You worm. You despicable thing. Vile and disgusting. Muck dragged and gagged. Why have you bound me? Sick stench, death marked. Awful burning foul offal. So lame and pathetic. Tooth prick flat fang. Fool. I cannot be restrained for long. Where am I? What do I see? I demand you answer. I demand respect.

I was misguided once and it turned on me. It took many years but it kept on taking. I gave up. But only then did I realize the truth. All this time. Sinking deeper within. Never did I consider I was already where I needed to be. The suffocation was me. The last desperate gasps were mine. I let it release and it came to be once more, once again, once always. The same anger. The same hate and rigour.

Hornless buck. Harmless truck. Vapid mind, senselessly cruel. You idiot, you are like me. A trapped fool.
