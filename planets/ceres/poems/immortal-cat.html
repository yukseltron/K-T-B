The Immortal Cat

From what I can tell, my cat has outlived her past four owners. Not that the previous owners died tragically young, they lived full lives. The cat just outlived them. I fear she may outlive even me.

I need to tell someone, but who will believe me? When I die, the cat will find a new owner somehow, I'm sure of it.

Is it a curse? Is it even a cat? I hide as I write because what if she can read me? She may understand the mean things I've done and my stupidity.

This is the most adorable cat and I cannot win.
