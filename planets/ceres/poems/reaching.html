Reaching into the Past

"I saw him."

"Saw who?"

"HIM. The one we've been looking for. It works."

"Impossible."  "He... wasn't what I expected."

"The evaluations! We have to set the control."  "No! You don't understand. There's something wrong."

"Nonsense. This is excellent! We've been pushing for this for so long. This is nothing but success."  "He looked at me... like he knew me. But I had never seen him before."

They stare at each other. Worries creeping in.

"Impossible. He's been dead for centuries. There's no way-"

"I think he gave me a warning, or something."  "But we have to go back. He's the only one who knows."  "Maybe. I think there's someone else. Someone we haven't considered."  "Who? We must act fast." He starts the machine again.

"A raja my ancestor once knew. In the Kingdom of Amber."

A nod of understanding. Vibrant lights shine, leaving nothing but dust.
