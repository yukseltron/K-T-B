Ablution

It lurches forward, stuck out and slack. Like oil poured down. The grease engrosses me.

I feel the grime between me gripped away. A quick chill. The steam lifts.

Re-energized. My metal sheen shines through. Gears slicked, it was clean enough.

I run. I can almost soar. The particles slip off me. "ABREACT!"

They consider it a ritual. Your process is that of a force. Rip away the filth you happen to carry.

But this is temporary. I can already feel it accumulate again. A restoration that I need, it will recommence.
