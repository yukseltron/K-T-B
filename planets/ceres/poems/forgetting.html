Do you remember it? No. What was your dream like? I said, no. There was nothing. Why can't you remember? I-what? I remember nothing about... Do you feel like you do remember? No... yes, something. Maybe. I'm not so- Describe it to me. No, NO! Describe it to me. I said... yes. Of course. Describe it to me. Yes. It ostensibly drives me and I express it through esoteric means. Why? Because, it-uh. The hyper... hypero... hypo... Why? I-I can't seem to remember. I'm sorry. Fine.
