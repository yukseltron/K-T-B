The Esoteric Plane

Of the seven thousand lokahs, I have but only seen several. Of the three hundred worlds, Only three, tilokah, have seen me. Of ten rupah, scattered as sand, I have yet to find any. He searched the beach. Hands dried and dirty. "Somewhere, here. It has to be." Washed up, bleached, and tarnished. A cryptic map, a broken compass, a golden coin. He searches the beach. Lips torn and bleeding. "3.45176869.68121.241234..."

Resonate. Elevate. Venerate. Eviscerate. Resuscitate.

The dreams stuck within your ambitious curse.
