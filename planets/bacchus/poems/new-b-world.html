New B. World

I wake up naturally in the morning around 7:00am. No alarms. I see in my phone tracker it's another 7.89 hour sleep. 100 minutes in REM. Clean and shower. 15 minutes of Yoga, 5 extra minutes in Shavasana. I set my intention, micro-goals, and timeline for the day. Wireless bluetooth ear pods are on. It's go time.

I check my crypto wallet and stocks. 5% in the green for options. Another 9% on my ETH-lite coins, with 5% total up from my derivatives. I do the work for the day; Minting my retouched AI-generated images as NFTs on a consortium blockchain. I check my emails, and ask a chat AI to write some friendly replies or set times compatible with my capacity.

For lunch, I put on my VR headset and enter my VR metaverse subworld populated with people from my federated Mastodon server. I compose some short-format videos and upload them to the #funni channel. I assess the interaction metrics and check their algorithmic-approval.

The afternoon is spent wrapping up work. I then go workout and track my health and progress through my smart watch. It pings me recommendations for today's routine. It also advises which macros I still need to reach and how. I place an order online for a contactless delivery.

Evenings, it's time to socialize outside. I'm taking pottery classes with a few of my friends. I later put my creations on sale through my self-hosted and self-made no-code web3 e-shop. It's part of my side-hustle.

When it's finally night time, I go home. I browse my streaming subs to see what's new and popular to watch. Afterwards it's time for bed. I put on meditative sleep sounds on my home speaker and ask it to turn off the lights. Another day awaits me.
