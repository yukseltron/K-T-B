RVNG pt.2

I drive down the very same road, the very next night.

"Find out what the fuck happened."

A man is missing. An important man at an important time. Now paranoia is in the air. Who ordered the hit? How did they know his identity? It's my job to find out.

"I can only trust you."

I track his phone. Deep in the city, in a small street-side cafe. It's almost empty when I get there. But the glass doors are locked. I unlock them, one way or another. I find the phone with the shopkeeper, so I take him with me.

"Use whatever you want."

I make him talk. He confesses while choking on a wet rag. He pleads for his life. I leave it up to chance. My next lead. I go through the motions. Kidnap, interrogate, jeopardize. This time I get a name.

"Get this shit done."

I find him. I wait for him. I follow him. He drives to an abandoned warehouse. I kill him swiftly.

"Fuck up anybody responsible."

I find another dead body inside the warehouse. Slumped in a corner of a shabby room. Mutilated. The code on his nape confirms it's him. I grieve. I wish I treated you better. It's all too late so soon.

All I can do now, is get your revenge.
