RVNG pt.1

We see him, outside and alone. The hot afternoon sun setting. He's smoking a cigarette and browsing his phone. He can't see us, parked a few meters behind him in the shade. It's time to strike.

A quick approach. He hears the car door open and turns around. I can see him calculating us but he doesn't realize what we're about to do. I grab his arm, pull him towards me. He trips and falls towards us. We grab him and muffle him as he tries to yell. His feet and hands are bound as we stuff him in the trunk.

"This is good. That was smooth."

It was much easier than any of us expected.

***

We've been torturing him for hours now. Four fingernails ripped out. Two fingers broken. Three knife stabs to the right quad. A torn left ear. One molar and three incisors ripped out. Probably two broken ribs, and a broken right femur. Four left toes crushed. And a face pummelled into a purple pulpy mash.

Yet... he hasn't said a word. No yell of pain, no begging, no fear. All we hear is his laboured breathing. We're losing patience.

"Why won't you fucking talk? Fucking idiot!"

Another swing at his head, this time we hear a much louder CRACK. He's gonna die now. A waterfall of blood is gushing from his head. Maybe he realizes it, because he finally says something.

"Haha *cough*, wait till... it's my turn he he."

It's his last words. What a fucking waste of time.
