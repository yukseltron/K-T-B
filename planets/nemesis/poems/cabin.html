Cabin

I left you there, trash. Ten long years. Are you still alive? Will you still scream when I beat you again? I'm coming back, you know. I am coming back again for you.

Every time, you ask me the same things - same stupid things. I don't know you. You're trapped here. You're alone. You're mine. I know what you'll do, I've seen what you can do. Trust me, you deserve this. I do too.

Broken glass. Gnawed wood. Raw blood. It festers, and I arrive. Another ten years to go, but I'll make sure to visit.
