Cured

A faint abyss lurks for me. Sensing my existence, it becomes my shape and form. My cradle of an eternal background. My escape catalyzes into me into catatonia.

A steep spill lurches me forward ten thousand steps twelve times. There it goes again, at same time it always does. I feel helpless to stop it. Continuous motions of chaos.

So strong you used to be. So weak I fear you become. Generosity gets limited around this time. Sympathetic to it all, I cast back the causes of your concerns.
