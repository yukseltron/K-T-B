Diaspora

Belittle the unknown and you are craven.

Be little of the unknown and you are craving.

Either way, I am stuck. Forcibly before I even realized when, where, and why. A journey transpiring over eons.

A billion years and I was dust. A million years and I was animated. A thousand years and I am predicted. A hundred years and I am formed. Ten years and I am born. A year and I am here.
