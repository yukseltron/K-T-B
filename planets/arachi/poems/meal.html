Meal

The cured meat I cooked. The hot skewer rotating gently over the hearth. Fresh smoke from the sizzle of the slow dripping fat. A crisp fire burning on groaning wood. But soon, a delicious meal.

She whispers into the water. A drink from the cool pool. The clear taste comforts her dizzying head. The clouds above offering a rushing cold. The draft pushes smoke back her way. She coughs.

It watches from far away. Hidden in the bush and shrouded by the night. The aroma was so alluring, it apologizes. Eager drools over sharp teeth. Large eyes waiting for an invite. Such gaunt hunger.
