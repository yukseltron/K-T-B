Arrival

The muezzin sends the summoning. It travels around the visage, all the way to its intricate facade on the other side. Inside, the prayers prostate themselves in worship.

Deeper in lies the great ruby and turquoise-tiled baths. They draw aquamarine water into its supple pools deep as any Cheshmeh. A cool air gently breezes through the lazy palms.

The Mamluk rests on top of the citadel, bored. While observing the people in the bazaar, his thoughts wander on having a cold stout drink to ease the summer heat.

He spots a caravan arriving in the distance. Leading it was a wali, surrounded by a royal guard and processions of merchants and traders.

The porte lifts its iron gates.
