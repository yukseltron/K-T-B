Fang

I catch its teeth. Long crescent daggers. A flash of crimson and tearing flesh. Rip, shred, crunch. Bored bones break. Blood soup.

Eventually, I let it happen. I am the loser. I am its meat. A low growl placates.

So close, I can smell the fur. Long grey mixed with black and white. Soft, forceful touch against me.

Naked and consumed.
