Pseudepigrapha

Hashura, the wise, the noble, the brave. How strong is your ways? Deyva, the just, the bold, the kind. How long will you stay? From above, to below. From cosmsic rumination and ruination, ancient powers push and pull. Belligerent malevolence, diligent benevolence Ahrim, the hypostatic. Lies in anthroposophic Ariyam, the venerated. Ex-votos consecrated An eternity for divinity.
