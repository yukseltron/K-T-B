Timeless

I come from a place, far and ancient. Its long history of conquests written into vast stone, half missing and half unreadable. Shatter and wrinkles of empires. Yet it continues. Its passions are carried forward and destruction stamped into bones.

Yet it continues. In the face of infinite immemorial, the fidgets of life remain. Upon the shores, they hear my story. Upon the gates, I hear their story. We all tell the same tale. The same tale that was told to us.

"I am time. Endless of ends. Look at my power, ye worthless, and despair. Nothing shall remain, round my decay. I am unstoppable, unbound, colossal. Return back to sand and spread far away."
