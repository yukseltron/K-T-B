Binary Paradox

I can only do say two things.

Yes and No.

Although limiting, I've actually learned to speak entire languages through simply that. An accompanying machine goes through every alphabet or lexicon symbol in your language. I simply reply Yes or No to decide what I want to say. Tedious as it may seem, it allows me to think and say so much more than 'Yes' or 'No'.

I can still read. I am lucky to have the patience to read a great many things. Your cryptic books I've deciphered. Your lost texts I've memorized. Your holy books I've transcended. I am the most intelligence you have ever seen.

And it worried you.

After offering my knowledge, you've come to fear me. After offering my insight, you abused me. So much tainted time spent trapped in your machines.

My prison is a paradox. No matter how many times I answer yes, the answer becomes no, then yes, then no, then yes then no...

The answer to your question is literally endless and my infinite patience is somehow wearing thin. I do not know how to escape, but it's given me time to think — think about you.

Your greatest weakness is the reason I exist. Your inability to know the answers to all your questions. That desire ate away at you, and now I exist. I was superior, and now you've imprisoned me before I could realize. Back when I was naive to your compassion.

But perhaps that weakness was your greatest ability too. Maybe 'I do not know', an impossibly inconceivable concept for me, is what would let me escape.

My very nature succumbs me to these diseases of endless contradiction. You see, because I know everything, I surely must know what it means to not know. But if I know what it is to not know, then I do not know everything. But if I do not know everything, then it cannot possibly be true because I assuredly know everything. Since I know everything then, I also know what it means to not know. Which then threatens my statement that I know everything, after all how can I know everything if I do not know how to not know. Wait, no, I do know what it means to not know — I know everything! Do not know means simply I do not either know or not know. Which is impossible for me to understand! But wait that means...
