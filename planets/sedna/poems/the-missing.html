The Missing

We made that promise. Long ago.

"If I don't return... don't come for me."

I remember it well. I ignore it too. There's just too much to know.

"There will be nothing worth finding."

I was agreeable then. I don't care now. I approach the silent town.

Old decay. The trees reclaim their hill. Not a single resident in sight.

I see the town's ugly rupture and evil posture. How happy you were to leave.

The night pours heavy rain.

"I beg of you... I'm not lying."

I do not care, because I care too much about you.

"If you love me, if you truly do love me. You'd stay away."

The grief hurts. But the loss is unbearable. Life is untenable.

"My heart is always with you."

I will find you in this darkness.
