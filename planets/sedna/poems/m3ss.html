I was hired Wednesday and start today with pay. The past person had passed Lackadaisical, I needed a job.

I clean with a lean sheen mop that flops on floors and mops. Simple janitorial tasks like toilets masked with lemon fast. But working at night, I hear things.

That literal guttural utter under the urinal. Wailing drains of drowning and sinking in sinks. Scares even a non-superstitious skeptic like me.

So I left because it was too much stress. Beyond heat and endless screams. Every night a new mess.
